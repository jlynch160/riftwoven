You & Your Reflection

When the Moonlit Mirror cracked, your reflection stepped out — and got everything backwards. You two are opposites in every way, and the Rift wants you kept apart across a kaleidoscope of worlds: arcane, medieval, clockwork, neon, cosmic, and ashen. Reunite, and you become Riftwoven — able to walk them all. Five companions and one rival react to every choice you make.

⇄ Set who you are; your reflection locks to the exact inverse. Pick who you play — the other walks beside you as your AI companion.

Azure OpenAI — Live Loom

Optional. With a deployment configured, every agent becomes a real LLM call (Game Master interpret → character fan-out → narration). Leave blank to run the bulletproof offline Loom — the demo behaves identically either way.

With an image deployment set, AI portraits of you & your reflection are generated during the prologue. Without it, stylized procedural avatars are used.

📚 Foundry IQ — hosted lore retrieval (optional)
🧩 MCP server — campaign memory tool (optional)
🗣 Azure AI Speech — neural narration (optional)

The Game Master & lore-keeper agents call consult_lore (Foundry IQ), web_search, and campaign_memory (MCP/Storage) as Azure function-calling tools. Hosted endpoints upgrade those tools from the built-in index to real Foundry IQ / MCP; both fall back gracefully.

⚠ Demo convenience: the key lives in localStorage and calls go straight to Azure from the browser. For production, proxy through a backend.
